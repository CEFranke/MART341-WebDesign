How to Make a Good Cup of London Fog

By: Caitlyn Franke

Ingredients

  • 1 Bag of Earl Grey Tea
  • 3oz of Water
  • 3oz of Warm Milk
  • Sugar/Sweetener of preferance
  • 1-3 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • Optional: Lavender Extract

Directions

  1. Heat up your water to about 90oC (or 190oF)
  2. Steep the tea in the hot water for around 3-5 minuets
  3. Heat up your milk to about 60oC (or 140oF). Be careful to NOT over heat the milk.
  4. Combine the tea and warm milk together in a mug that can hold 6oz.
  5. Stir in your prefered sugar or sweetener to personal preferance. 1-3 teaspoons recomended
  6. Stir in about 1-2 teaspoons of Vanilla Extract
  7. Optional: Stir in lavender extract to preferance
