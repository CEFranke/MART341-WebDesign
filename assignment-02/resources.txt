What is a directory, and what is its relationship to files?

A directory is where files are stored, 
and directories can have 'parent-child' relations to each other with the one in the higher hierarchical position being the parent.


How are VCS useful?

Version Control Systems are extremely useful in ways of knowing how your system has progressed, what changes were made, 
and most importantly to have something you can rely on to revert to if a catostrophic mistake breaks the whole system to an 'un-repairable' state.
