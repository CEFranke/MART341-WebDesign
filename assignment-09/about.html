Why Was This Site Made?

I, Caitlyn, just really like rocks. I own my own rock collection back home. While I personally just seek out rocks that happen to be very shiny, very pretty, or just kinda cool to me, I also have interest in the general cost of gems because I do have to buy them. Like how I own a ruby, which would sound very expensive, except that it's an uncut gem that is not polished and is still stuck partly in a rock.
